Notre Dame Trumpets

~ Carrying the Melody Since 1845 ~

The University of Notre Dame Marching Band is in it 167th year of continuous service to the University, making it the nation's oldest university band. Long live Schmed. The Trumpets are but one section in the full 405-member University of Notre Dame Marching Band. As the largest and best section, we have a lot to uphold.
