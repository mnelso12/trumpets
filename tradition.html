Trumpet Caroling

Every Christmas season we go caroling at the Saint Mary's convent for the adorable old nuns. Their favorite Christmas carol is the Vicotry March. Actually though.

Fall Break Service Trip

Every Fall Break we have an optional band service trip. We do everything - help with ESL classes, make houses, clean kitchens, visit homeless shelters, and play for lots and lots of kiddos.

Bandlink

For 34 years, we've given trumpet lessons to kids in the South Bend area without music programs of their own.
